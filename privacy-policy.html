This policy applies to information collected through www.timber.to, the Timberly application, and related services used to capture operational messages, media, and business events. If you use Timberly through an employer or customer organization, that organization may control some of the business data processed through the service.

2. Information We Collect

  • Business data such as production events, stock movements, order updates, dispatch information, and shift activity.
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, logs, timestamps, and diagnostic events.

3. How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and secure the Timberly service.
  • Process WhatsApp and other inbound messages into operational records and workflows.

  • Communicate with users about support, service updates, security issues, and account administration.
  • Monitor performance, prevent abuse, investigate incidents, and improve product quality.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.
